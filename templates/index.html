Our Story

From the Heart of the Himalayas

Founded in 2018, Kandamul was born from a passion to share Nepal's incredible culinary heritage with the world while supporting local farming communities.

We work directly with small-scale farmers across Nepal's diverse regions, from the lush valleys to the high mountains, ensuring fair compensation and sustainable practices.

Every product in our collection tells a story of tradition, culture, and the loving hands that crafted it. We're more than just food – we're a bridge connecting you to the authentic tastes and traditions of Nepal.
